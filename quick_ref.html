Player Turns

Players may take their turns each round in any order. During a turn each player may take one of the following actions:

  • Move
  • Retool their ship (for mining or building )
  • Mine* a resource (if tooled for mining and at a resource planet)
  • Build* a structure (if tooled for building and carying sufficient resources)
  • Upgrade a ship (if tooled for building and colocated with the ship being upgraded). Only an upgraded ship () can transport water
They may also do the following at any time:
  • Re-fuel
  • Transfer fuel or resources between colocated ships

* Players may also roll the dice once to try to gain a bonus build or mine action. The roll can have the following outcomes:
  • 1 or 2 - Their tool breaks and they will need to retool before building or mining again.
  • 3 or 4 - The attempt fails
  • 5 or 6 - The attempt succeeds and the player can proceed with the action

Players indicate the have used their action (or are choosing not to) by turning their action token face down. When all action tokens are face down, everything orbits one space counter clockwise, players flip their action tokens up again, and a new round begins.

Winning or Losing

Winning

Players win by collecting a sufficient amount of ice () and transporting it to the target planet.

  • Easy: One for each player playing.
  • Normal: Two for each player.
  • Hard: Three for each player.

Losing

The players lose if the colony ship reaches the target planet before sufficient ice is delivered.
