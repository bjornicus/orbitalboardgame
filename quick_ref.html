Rounds

  1. Pirate turn: move each pirate piece or harvest pirate resources as described in the 'Pirate Turn' section.
  2. Player turns: each player takes a turn as described in the 'Player Turns' section.
  3. Orbit: move all ships, planets, stations, and pirates counter clockwise one space.
  4. Recycle tokens: Return any spent action tokens or destroyed pirates to the draw bag.

Pirate Turns

  1. Move
  2. Pillage

Player Turns

  1. Draw
  2. Play:
    • Refit
    • Re-fuel
    • Move(once per turn)
    • Trade
    • Special Actions(one per action token)

Movement Cost

  • Changing orbits, arriving at a space with no planet - 1/2 of your total carried mass (fuel + cargo units currently on board your ship)
  • Changing orbits, arriving at a planet - 1/4 of your total carried mass
  • Changing orbits, departing from an acceleration ring - 1/4 of your total carried mass
  • Changing orbits, departing from an acceleration ring and arriving at a planet - 1/8 of your total carried mass.
  • Moving within the same orbit)- 1 fuel unit

Special Actions

  • Extract resources from a planet when in orbit.
  • Destroy a pirate on the same space.
  • Move an extra space
  • Build
  • Upgrade a ring or station
  • Recycle a ring or station
  • Refit any ship on the same space (including itself)
  • Boost an empty ring or station (normal movement cost +3). Your ship may not re-fuel on the same turn.
  • Move an extra space

Winning or Losing

Winning

  • Easy: Successfully construct and provision one orbital habitat.
  • Normal: Successfully construct and provision two orbital habitats.
  • Hard: Successfully construct and provision three orbital habitats.

Losing

  • Easy: Pirates collect ten resource tokens.
  • Normal: Pirates collect eight resource tokens..
  • Hard: Pirates collect six resource tokens..
